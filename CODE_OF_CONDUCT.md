# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want `portallib` to be a useful, welcoming project for researchers, engineers, and contributors
of every background and experience level.

Expected behavior includes:

- communicating clearly and respectfully;
- giving specific, constructive feedback;
- accepting correction and taking responsibility for mistakes;
- respecting different viewpoints, boundaries, and privacy; and
- keeping technical disagreements focused on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, unwanted sexual attention,
publishing another person's private information, sustained disruption, or personal attacks.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces
where someone is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting and enforcement

Use GitHub's private **Report content** flow on the relevant issue, comment, or pull request to
report unacceptable behavior to the repository administrators. Do not repeat sensitive personal
information in a public issue.

Maintainers may edit or remove content and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation
when behavior conflicts with this standard. Reports will be reviewed in context and handled as
privately as the platform allows.
